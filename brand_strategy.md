## 🎨 STEP 2: LUXURY DESIGN SYSTEM

**1. Color Palette**
* **Primary (60%):** Cloud White (`#FAFAFA`) & Alabaster (`#F2F0EB`) – Creates a clean, breathable clinical canvas.
* **Secondary (30%):** Deep Slate Black (`#1A1A1A`) – For high-contrast, premium typography.
* **Accent (10%):** Liquid Gold (`#C5A059`) & Muted Sage (`#A9BCA4`) – For subtle CTA elegance and botanical nods.

**2. Typography**
* **Heading Font:** *Ogg*, *Playfair Display*, or *GT Alpina* – An elegant, high-end editorial serif.
* **Body Font:** *Inter*, *Neue Haas Grotesk*, or *Helvetica Neue* – Ultra-clean, highly legible sans-serif.
* **Hierarchy:** Massive, light-weight headings; small, tracked-out uppercase subheadings (letter-spacing: 2px); readable, airy body copy (line-height: 1.6).

**3. UI Rules**
* **Spacing:** Massive whitespace. Let the products and imagery breathe.
* **Section Padding:** 100px–140px on desktop; 60px–80px on mobile.
* **Border Radius:** `0px` or `2px`. Sharp, editorial edges scream high-end. Avoid bubbly, round corners.
* **Images & Hover:** No cheap drop shadows. Use a subtle, cinematic image slow-zoom on hover (1.03x scale, 0.6s ease).

**4. CTA Design**
* **Button Color:** Deep Slate Black (`#1A1A1A`) with Cloud White text. 
* **Hover Behavior:** Fade to 80% opacity or invert to Champagne Gold.
* **Text Examples:** "Experience Radiance," "Shop The Formula," "Curate Your Routine."

## ⚙️ STEP 6: CONVERSION PSYCHOLOGY

* **Sticky Add-to-Cart:** A sleek bar at the bottom or top of the screen that appears once scrolling past the main ATC button on mobile.
* **Trust Badges:** Ditch the ugly colored payment icons. Use sleek SVG line-icons near the ATC: "Dermatologist Tested | 30-Day Efficacy Guarantee | Secure Checkout"
* **Social Proof:** Clean star ratings directly under the product title (e.g., `★★★★★ 4.9/5 | 128 Reviews`).
* **Micro-Urgency:** "Only 12 batches left in current production run." (More premium than "HURRY 3 LEFT!").
* **Anchoring:** Use the Compare At price on singular products to anchor value.
* **Upsells (In-Cart):** "Pairs Perfectly With..." (e.g., suggest the Cleanser when buying the Serum).
* **The Bundle Offer:** "The Core Radiance Bundle" – Buy all 3 steps for $160 (Value $190).

---

## 📱 STEP 7: UX & MOBILE OPTIMIZATION

* **Mobile-First Header:** Sticky header with a crisp hamburger menu to the left, logo center, and cart (with notification dot) right.
* **Thumb-Friendly:** Make the "Add to Cart" and "Checkout" buttons span 100% of the screen width on mobile so they are impossible to miss.
* **Clean Layout via Accordions:** Use accordions on the Product Page for "Ingredients," "How to Use," and "Shipping" to save vertical space.
* **Image Gallery:** Enable swipeable product images with pagination dots.

## 🔌 STEP 8: APPS & TOOLS

* **Reviews:** **Okendo** (Highly premium, allows customers to list attributes like "Skin Type: Dry" or "Age: 30-40", building massive trust).
* **Bundles / Upsells:** **Kaching Bundles** or **Rebuy Engine** for elegant, native-looking quantity breaks and in-cart cross-sells.
* **Email Marketing:** **Klaviyo**. Essential for the "Welcome Series" (Inner Circle) and "Abandoned Cart" flows.
* **Search:** **Shopify Search & Discovery** (Native) for typo-tolerance and predictive dropdowns.
* **Analytics:** **Microsoft Clarity** (Free tool to watch heatmaps and screen recordings of mobile users to see where they struggle).

---

## 🧪 STEP 9: TESTING FLOW

* **The Path to Purchase:** Manually test Homepage -> Product -> ATC -> Cart -> Checkout on both an iPhone and Desktop.
* **Discounts:** Verify that the "15% off Welcome" code actually applies at checkout, but doesn't double-stack with bundles.
* **Shipping Threshold:** Ensure the "Free Shipping over $100" logic triggers correctly when the cart hits $100.
* **Link Audit:** Click every single link in the footer and main menu. No dead ends. No generic `/collections/frontpage` links.

---

## 🎯 STEP 10: FINAL POLISH & QA

* [ ] **No Placeholder Content:** Remove all "Powered by Shopify" branding in the footer.
* [ ] **Brand Consistency:** Ensure buttons are 100% uniform (same border-radius and padding everywhere).
* [ ] **Image Optimization:** All product photos must be compressed (TinyPNG) and crop-aligned to exactly the same aspect ratio (e.g., 4:5 vertical).
* [ ] **Copy QA:** Every paragraph checks out as premium, correctly spelled, and emotionally tuned.
* [ ] **Mobile Alignment:** No copy should be wrapping awkwardly on mobile (e.g., single orphaned words on a line).
